The Best Locations in Zanzibar

Nungwi is located on the northern tip of the island and is home to a great variety of beach bars and clubs.
Recommended Hotels (STANDARD GARDEN VIEW/DOUBLE ROOM/B&B): Amaan Beach Bungalows (3*) around $94/£80pn,
Z Hotel (4*) & Nungwi Beach Resort by Turaco (4*) around $300/£240pn.

Kendwa a more relaxed area to is neighbour Nungwi provides stunning white sandy beaches & blue waters.
Recommended Hotel (STANDARD GARDEN VIEW/DOUBLE ROOM/B&B): Kendwa Rocks Hotel (3*) around $175/£122pn.

NOTE: The Government Infrastructure Tax (City Tax) in Zanzibar is $5/£4 per person per night

Q&A

Q:Best Location to Stay in Zanzibar?

A:Nungwi, located north of the Island.

Q:What is the culture in Zanzibar?

A:Primarily Muslim culture! So you must be modestly dressed and avoid drinking alcohol publicly unless in reserved areas such as bars and hotels.

Q:How much are airport transfers for 7-10 ppx?

A:$90/£70 per car on-way.

Q:Transportation to Zanzibar from Dar Es Salaam?

A:Azam Marine Ferry VIP Seats: $120pp/£100pp Return / Domestic Flight: £60+ Return.

Q:Which airport do I arrive in when in Dar Es Salaam & Zanzibar?

A:Dar Es Salaam: Julius Nyerere International Airport & Zanzibar: Abeid Amani Karume International Airport.
