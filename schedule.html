A: Yes. You will need a ORDINARY (SINGLE ENTRY 90 Days) VISA. Costs $50 Cash on Arrival.

A: Please seek independent advice from your healthcare professional.

Q: How to Pay Whilst in Tanzania?

A: The local currency is Tanzania Shillings which can be withdrawn from the ATM. Many places also accept debit and credit cards but cash is king.
